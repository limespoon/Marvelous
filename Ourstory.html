JOLIZ'S STORY

I started watching Marvel as a kid when the X-MEN cartoon came out. In it, the X-men are ostracized by others for simply being who they are.. What was cool was that what made them different was what made them powerful. Since then, I've loved all the other Marvel stories, especially since so many are based in New York City. No matter which you watch, it shows that people at the end of the day, have flaws,but they do their best to be good and that's all you can ask for. Besides, the movies have some of the most fun soundtracks.
